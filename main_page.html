Task assignment

Round-robin task assignment algorithm where each team member receives an equal share of tasks to be completed daily. Allocate tasks in a fair and balanced manner, preventing any single team member from being overloaded with work while others have light loads. This approach helps optimize team productivity and prevents bottlenecks caused by uneven task distribution.

Task tracking & Task analysis

provides visibility into which tasks are in progress, completed, or pending, helping stakeholders stay updated on project progress and identify any potential bottlenecks.
